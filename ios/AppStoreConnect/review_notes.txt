REVIEW NOTES — for Apple App Review team

App overview:
RB Perform is a personalized sports coaching companion app. Athletes use it to receive their training program from a human coach, track their workouts (loads, reps, RPE), watch HD exercise videos, log nutrition (with barcode scanner), and message their coach directly.

ACCOUNT REQUIREMENT:
Yes — login is via passwordless magic link (email OTP). The app pairs an athlete with a coach. To review the full experience, please use the demo account below (already linked to a demo coach with a complete sample program).

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES USED:
• Supabase (auth + data, Frankfurt EU region)
• Vercel (web app delivery)
• Stripe (payments — billing happens on the coach side, not athletes)
• Zoho Mail (transactional emails)
• @capacitor/push-notifications (push) — using APNs

DATA COLLECTION & PRIVACY:
Disclosed via Privacy Manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy):
• Email (account, linked, App Functionality)
• Name (display, linked, App Functionality)
• Photos (weekly check-in body photos shared with coach, linked, App Functionality)
• Health & Fitness data (weight, RPE, performance metrics, linked, App Functionality)
• Device ID (APNs token for push, linked, App Functionality)
• Crash data (debug, not linked, App Functionality)
No tracking. No third-party ad networks. No data sale.
