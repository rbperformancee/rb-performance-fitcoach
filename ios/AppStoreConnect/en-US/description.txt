RB Perform is your personalized sports coaching, right on your iPhone.

Program built by a real coach (not AI), real-time tracking, HD videos for every exercise, custom nutrition, and direct messaging with your coach.

————————————

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Your weekly program, session by session
Strength, running, mobility, athletics — your complete plan adapted to your sport, level, and goals.

• Load, reps and RPE tracker
Log each set right when you do it. The app shows your progress week over week. Personal record (PR) detection that pings your coach when you hit a new max.

• HD video for every exercise
No more guessing on form — tap the exercise to see the demo filmed by your coach.

• Smart rest timer
Automatic start after each set. Push notification when it's time to go again, even with the app closed.

• Built-in 1RM test
Type "3RM" in the reps field — the app calculates your estimated 1RM using the Epley formula after your set.

• Nutrition + barcode scanner
Log meals in 30 seconds. Built-in barcode scanner for packaged foods. Personalized calorie and protein goals based on your sport.

• Direct coach messaging
Text, voice and photo messages with your coach between sessions. Responses within 24 business hours.

• Weekly check-in
Progress photos + weight + how you feel. Your coach sees the trend and adapts the program.
