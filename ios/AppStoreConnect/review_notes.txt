REVIEW NOTES — for Apple App Review team

App overview:
RB Perform is a personalized sports coaching companion app. Athletes use it to receive their training program from a human coach, track their workouts (loads, reps, RPE), watch HD exercise videos, log nutrition (with barcode scanner), and message their coach directly.

ACCOUNT REQUIREMENT:
Yes — login is via passwordless magic link (email OTP). The app pairs an athlete with a coach. To review the full experience, please use the demo account below (already linked to a demo coach with a complete sample program).

The demo account is pre-seeded with rich realistic data:

Reviewer-visible features in this demo:
• Tab "Train" — view the PPL program, browse exercises, see history
• Tab "Body" — weight chart with 30-day trend, goal setting
• Tab "Fuel" — nutrition tracking + barcode scanner permission flow
• Tab "Run" — activity log + Apple Health integration prompt
• Tab "Profile" — settings, account deletion, message thread with coach

The demo account is protected server-side: account deletion is simulated
(returns success without actually deleting) so reviewers cannot break it
for subsequent testers. A daily cron (/api/cron-demo-reset @ 03:00 UTC)
re-seeds the data to keep the demo fresh.

ACCOUNT DELETION:
